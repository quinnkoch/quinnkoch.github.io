Proof that the Earth is Flat

This is a simulation for the N-Body problem. The two small cicles are suns, you can click on a location to move one of the suns there, and when you press "a" the other sun will move to the location of your mouse. The circles represent planets, and all are attracted by the suns' gravity, as well as to each other. The smaller planets move fastest, and they all change color based on their speed.
